Responsible Gaming: Setting Limits and Staying Safe in 2026

Practical guide to maintaining healthy gaming habits. Learn how to set limits, recognize warning signs, and access support resources.

Setting Deposit Limits

Most reputable Indian gaming platforms offer deposit limit options. Here's how to use them effectively:

  • Daily limits — Set a maximum amount you can deposit each day
  • Weekly limits — Broader budget control for longer periods
  • Monthly limits — Overall budget cap for comprehensive management

Set these limits when you first start playing, before any losses occur. Limits can only be increased after a cooling-off period (usually 24-72 hours) to prevent impulsive changes.

Time Management

  • Set an alarm or timer for each gaming session
  • Take a 15-minute break every hour of continuous play
  • Never play when tired, stressed, or emotionally vulnerable
  • Schedule gaming sessions rather than playing impulsively
  • Consider using app timers or screen time controls on your device

Warning Signs of Problematic Gaming

Watch for these indicators:

  • Spending more money or time on gaming than intended
  • Chasing losses by playing more to recover money
  • Lying to friends or family about gaming activity
  • Neglecting work, studies, or relationships due to gaming
  • Feeling irritable or anxious when unable to play
  • Gaming to escape problems or negative emotions
  • Borrows money or sells possessions to fund gaming

Support Resources in India

  • iCall — Free helpline: 9152987821 (Mon-Sat, 8am-10pm)
  • GamCare India — gamcareindia.org
  • Nasha Mukt Bharat — Government addiction helpline

Most platforms also have self-exclusion options where you can voluntarily block your account for a set period ranging from 1 week to permanently.
