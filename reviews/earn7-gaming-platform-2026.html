What is Earn7?

Earn7 is a newer entrant in the Indian real-money gaming market, positioning itself as a skill-based gaming platform with quiz games, trivia contests, and mini-games. The platform targets a younger demographic with casual gaming experiences and low entry barriers.

Our 30-day test account was created and monitored from March 1 to March 10, 2026, covering account creation, KYC, gameplay across available games, deposits, and withdrawal requests.

Game Library — 7.2/10

Earn7's game library is more limited than established competitors:

  • Quiz Games — Multiple trivia categories including general knowledge, sports, movies, and current affairs
  • Memory Games — Pattern matching and memory challenge games
  • Speed Games — Quick-reaction games with leaderboard competitions
  • Word Games — Crossword-style and word search contests
  • Color Games — Simple prediction-based mini-games

While the game selection is more limited than MPL or WinZO, the quiz and trivia categories are well-designed with good question variety. The platform's focus on quick-session games makes it suitable for casual players who want short gaming bursts.

Payment Methods — 7.8/10

Supported payment methods include:

  • UPI — Primary payment method, reliable processing
  • Paytm — Wallet-based deposits
  • Bank Transfer — Direct transfers for deposits and withdrawals

Minimum deposit of ₹50 makes it accessible for new players. Payment processing for deposits was reliable during our test period with no failed transactions.

Withdrawal Process — 7.8/10

Withdrawal testing results:

  • UPI Withdrawals — Processed within 1-4 hours. Average: 2 hours.
  • Bank Transfer — 1-2 business days
  • Minimum Withdrawal — ₹150

Withdrawals were processed without issues during our test period. The platform uses standard anti-fraud checks including deposit-to-withdrawal ratio monitoring.

KYC Process — 7.5/10

Earn7's KYC process:

  1. Step 1 — Mobile OTP verification
  2. Step 2 — Aadhaar-based eKYC
  3. Step 3 — PAN card verification
  4. Step 4 — Bank account linking

eKYC processing took about 12 minutes in our test — slightly longer than industry leaders but acceptable. Full verification for unlimited transactions was completed within 24 hours.

Trust Score — 7.4/10

Earn7 is a newer platform (launched 2023-2024) and lacks the track record of established players. The company behind Earn7 is not publicly listed, and corporate transparency is limited. User reviews on app stores are mixed, with some players reporting successful experiences and others citing slower withdrawals than advertised.

The platform has been operating without major regulatory issues, but the shorter operating history means a lower trust score compared to established platforms like RummyCircle or MPL.

Verdict — 7.6/10

Earn7 is a viable option for casual players who enjoy quiz games and trivia contests. The platform's strength lies in its quick-session gameplay and accessible entry point. However, the limited game library and shorter track record compared to competitors mean it doesn't yet compete with the top-tier platforms.

Pros: Easy entry point, good quiz content, reasonable bonus structure, UPI withdrawals work reliably

Cons: Limited game variety, newer platform with shorter history, lower trust score, no live chat support

Best for: Casual gamers, quiz enthusiasts, players new to real-money gaming looking for low-stakes options.
