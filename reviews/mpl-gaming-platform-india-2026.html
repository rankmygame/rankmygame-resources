What is MPL?

MPL (Mobile Premier League) is one of India's largest mobile gaming platforms, launched in 2018. The platform offers a wide variety of games including fantasy sports (cricket, football, kabaddi), rummy, and casual arcade games. As of 2026, MPL has grown to become one of the most recognizable names in the Indian real-money gaming space, competing directly with WinZO and other major platforms.

During our 30-day testing period, we created accounts on both Android and iOS, played across multiple game categories, deposited and withdrew funds, and contacted customer support multiple times to evaluate real-world performance.

Game Library — 8.5/10

MPL's game library is one of its strongest selling points. The platform offers over 60 games across several categories:

  • Fantasy Cricket — IPL, international matches, domestic T20 leagues. Full contest coverage with multiple prize tiers.
  • Fantasy Football — Premier League, La Liga, Serie A, ISL. Smaller contest pools compared to cricket but solid coverage.
  • Fantasy Kabaddi — Pro Kabaddi League matches. Good option for sports fans looking for alternatives to cricket.
  • Rummy — Points rummy, pool rummy, and deals rummy. Available 24/7 with multiple tables stakes levels.
  • Arcade Games — Cricket game, bubble shooter, fruit ninja-style games, and more casual options.
  • Quiz Games — Knowledge-based contests with real money prizes.

The fantasy sports experience is polished, with good contest variety and intuitive team selection. The rummy platform is well-designed with smooth card animations and responsive gameplay. Arcade games feel like lightweight time-killers rather than serious real-money offerings.

Payment Methods — 8.2/10

MPL supports a comprehensive range of deposit methods suitable for Indian players:

  • UPI — Immediate deposits, widely supported
  • Paytm Wallet — Popular instant payment option
  • PhonePe — Growing adoption among Indian users
  • Bank Transfer — Direct bank transfers, slightly slower
  • Debit/Credit Cards — Visa, Mastercard supported
  • Amazon Pay — Convenient for Amazon users

Minimum deposit is ₹50, making it accessible for casual players. Maximum deposit limits vary based on KYC verification level. We tested UPI deposits and funds were credited within 2 minutes in all test transactions.

Withdrawal Process — 8.2/10

Withdrawal is where MPL performs well in our tests. We requested withdrawals via UPI and bank transfer:

  • UPI Withdrawals — Processed within 30 minutes to 4 hours in our tests. Average: 1.5 hours.
  • Bank Transfer — Processed within 1-2 business days.
  • Minimum Withdrawal — ₹200 for bank transfer, ₹100 for UPI.

One important note: withdrawals are only permitted after completing full KYC verification. We encountered one withdrawal held for manual review (large amount), which took 18 hours to clear — this is standard practice for security but worth knowing.

KYC Process — 8.5/10

MPL's KYC process is streamlined and mostly automated:

  1. Step 1 — Mobile number verification via OTP
  2. Step 2 — Aadhaar-based eKYC (instant, automated)
  3. Step 3 — PAN card details for tax compliance
  4. Step 4 — Bank account verification (₹1 micro-transaction)

We completed eKYC in under 5 minutes. PAN verification took about 30 minutes during business hours. The automated eKYC system is one of the fastest we've seen in the industry.

Customer Support — 8.5/10

MPL offers multiple support channels:

  • In-app chat — Available 24/7, average response time under 2 minutes
  • Email support — Response within 4-6 hours
  • Help center — Comprehensive FAQ and guides
  • Social media — Active Twitter/X and Facebook pages

We tested support via in-app chat for deposit issues and withdrawal queries. Agents were knowledgeable and resolved our test issues within 10 minutes in most cases.

Verdict — 8.4/10

MPL is a solid all-rounder platform that excels in game variety and payment reliability. The platform's strength lies in its fantasy sports offerings, particularly cricket, where it competes head-to-head with Dream11. The rummy section is well-designed and the overall mobile experience is smooth.

Pros: Excellent game variety, fast UPI withdrawals, polished mobile app, 24/7 support, fast eKYC

Cons: Arcade games feel secondary to fantasy sports, bonus terms can be complex, some withdrawal holds for manual review

Best for: Fantasy sports enthusiasts (especially cricket), players who value fast withdrawals, mobile-first gamers.
