KYC Process Guide for Indian Gaming Platforms 2026 — Complete Verification Walkthrough

Everything you need to know about completing KYC verification on Indian real-money gaming platforms. Document requirements, step-by-step processes, and solutions for common issues.

Why KYC is Required

Indian law requires real-money gaming platforms to verify user identity for anti-money laundering (AML) compliance and age verification. KYC is not optional — it's a legal requirement that enables the platform to legally offer real-money gaming services.

KYC also protects you: verified accounts have higher transaction limits, faster withdrawal processing, and better security features.

Documents You'll Need

  • Aadhaar Card — For eKYC verification (most common and fastest method)
  • PAN Card — For tax compliance and identity verification
  • Bank Account — For withdrawal destination verification
  • Mobile Number — Linked to your Aadhaar for OTP verification

Standard KYC Process

Step 1: Mobile Verification

Register with your mobile number and verify via OTP. This creates your account and enables basic access.

Step 2: Aadhaar eKYC

Enter your 12-digit Aadhaar number and verify via OTP sent to your Aadhaar-linked mobile. The system validates your details against UIDAI records. Processing time: 5-15 minutes.

Step 3: PAN Verification

Enter your 10-character PAN number. The platform validates it against NSDL/CDSL databases. Processing time: 10 minutes to 2 hours depending on the platform and time of day.

Step 4: Bank Account Linking

Enter your bank account details (account number, IFSC code). The platform may send a small ₹1 micro-transaction to verify ownership. Processing time: instant to 24 hours.

Common KYC Issues and Solutions

Aadhaar OTP not received?
Ensure your mobile number is linked to your Aadhaar. Try again after 5 minutes. If the issue persists, visit a UIDAI enrollment center to update your linked mobile.
PAN validation failed?
Check for typos in your PAN number. Ensure your PAN is linked to your Aadhaar. You can verify PAN-Aadhaar linking on the income tax e-filing portal.
Bank account verification failed?
Double-check your IFSC code and account number. Ensure the account is in your name. Contact your bank to confirm account status.
KYC pending for more than 24 hours?
Contact platform support with your registered mobile number and a screenshot of your KYC status page.
Aadhaar showing different name?
Your gaming account name must match your Aadhaar name. Contact platform support — some platforms allow manual document upload as an alternative.
