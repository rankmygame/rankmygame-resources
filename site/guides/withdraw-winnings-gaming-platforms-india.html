How to Withdraw Winnings from Gaming Platforms in India — Complete Guide 2026

Everything Indian gamers need to know about cashing out winnings from real-money gaming platforms. Step-by-step instructions, processing times, and troubleshooting tips.

Before You Withdraw: Prerequisites

Most Indian gaming platforms require completed verification before allowing withdrawals:

  • Mobile number verification — Must be completed first
  • Aadhaar eKYC — Required for all platforms
  • PAN card verification — Mandatory for tax compliance
  • Bank account linking — Withdrawal destination must be verified

Without full KYC, most platforms restrict withdrawals to ₹200-500 per transaction. Completing all verification steps unlocks higher limits and faster processing.

Step-by-Step: UPI Withdrawal

  1. Open the gaming app and navigate to your wallet or balance section
  2. Tap "Withdraw" or "Cash Out" — usually prominently displayed in the wallet area
  3. Enter the amount you want to withdraw (must be above minimum threshold)
  4. Select UPI as the withdrawal method
  5. Enter your UPI ID (e.g., yourname@oksbi) — ensure it's correct to avoid failed transfers
  6. Confirm the withdrawal and wait for processing
  7. Check your UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm) for the credited amount

Tips: UPI withdrawals are the fastest method. Avoid requesting withdrawals during late night hours when NPCI servers may be slower. Double-check your UPI ID before confirming.

Step-by-Step: Bank Transfer Withdrawal

  1. Navigate to wallet/balance section
  2. Select "Withdraw" and choose "Bank Transfer"
  3. Enter the amount (usually ₹500+ minimum)
  4. Select your pre-linked bank account
  5. Confirm the withdrawal
  6. Funds typically arrive within 1-3 business days

Common Issues and Solutions

Withdrawal stuck pending for more than 24 hours?
Contact platform support via in-app chat. Check if your bank account details are correct and if there are any KYC-related holds on your account.
Withdrawal failed but amount deducted from wallet?
Most platforms auto-reverse failed withdrawals within 24-48 hours. If not reversed, contact support with your transaction ID.
Wrong UPI ID entered?
Contact support immediately with the correct UPI ID. Some platforms can intercept and redirect the transfer before it's processed.
Bank account not linked?
Complete bank account linking through the KYC section. This usually involves a small ₹1 micro-verification transaction.
Withdrawal amount above daily limit?
Check your account's withdrawal limits in the profile/settings section. Complete higher KYC tiers to increase limits.

Withdrawal Security Tips

  • Always withdraw to your own bank account or UPI ID — third-party withdrawals are not supported
  • Link and verify your bank account before making large deposits
  • Enable two-factor authentication on your gaming account
  • Keep your KYC documents up to date
  • Be aware of platform-specific cooling-off periods for new bank account links
