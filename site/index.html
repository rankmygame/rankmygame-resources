How We Review Platforms

Testing Process

Each platform undergoes a 30-day testing period by our team. We create accounts, make deposits, play games, request withdrawals, and interact with customer support to evaluate real-world performance.

Scoring Dimensions

  • Game Library — Variety, quality, and freshness of games
  • Payment Methods — Deposit and withdrawal options available
  • Withdrawal Speed — Average time from request to funds received
  • Trust Score — Platform history, regulatory compliance, user reports
  • User Experience — Interface design, navigation, mobile performance
  • Bonus Terms — Welcome bonuses, reload offers, and wagering requirements
  • Customer Support — Response time, helpfulness, and availability
  • Security — Data protection, encryption, and fraud prevention
  • Fair Play — Random number generation, anti-cheat measures

Editorial Independence

We do not accept payment from platforms for reviews or rankings. Our revenue comes from the main site rankmygame.net, and our editorial team makes all scoring decisions independently. We update reviews when platforms make significant changes.
